GeneNetwork is a framework for web based genetics launched in 1994 as The Portable Dictionary of the Mouse Genome (previously WebQTL).

Operated by Rob Williams, Lei Yan, Zachary Sloan, Arthur Centeno and Pjotr Prins.

Published in


GeneNetwork is supported by:

  • The UT Center for Integrative and Translational Genomics
  • NIAAA Integrative Neuroscience Initiative on Alcoholism (U01 AA016662, U01 AA013499, U24 AA013513, U01 AA014425)
  • NIDA, NIMH , and NIAAA (P20-DA 21131)
  • NCI MMHCC (U01CA105417) and NCRR BIRN (U24 RR021760)
